The Banana Beach Bar-Restaurant is a “must go” destination since 2000!

The ultimate setting for endless hours of relaxation on the beach, delicious meals on the sand, cool cocktails for the time when the sun sinks, and unique dinners under the moonlight! And all this, with the sequence of atmospheric music and the melody of the sea. Fully equipped with sun beds, umbrellas, friendly staff, a delicious menu for the beach and a full menu for the restaurant. The place for those who want to spend the day at sea.
