01 / SWIM

SWIM

The most famous beach of Naxos.

The beach of Agia Anna is the next beach just after Agios Prokopios. It is the most famous beach of Naxos, 6 km from the port of Naxos, 3 km from the airport of Naxos. The 70’s and 80′, the pristine beach of Agia Anna was the main destination on the island for free camping.

Today, St. Anna, still so beautiful, but now organized as well, offering visitors endless hours of relaxation with a summer music and cool cocktails. The crystal clear waters of the turquoise sea and the endless golden sands, will be the point of relaxation for you and carefree hours to dive and play for your children.

Come the morning, pick your sunbed

Pick your sunbed (choose one, right in front of the wave) and we will take care of all the rest…. We will arrange every detail..to offer you ultimate relaxation and serenity …

It is all you need during your holiday!
