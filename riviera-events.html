Elena & Andreas wedding styling

This type of setting is ideal to unite with your loved one, and summer is the best time of year for such an occasion. A beach wedding can be magical, romantic as well as enchanting, especially near the crystal clear Aegean waters.

Whether your style is traditional, rock, bohemian, vintage, casual or posh, here on Naxos we can cater to suit a variety of individual styles. You can have a wedding that will surely reflect your personality and identity. Naxos is undoubtedly a perfect wedding location as it offers a variety of venues to host this very special celebration for you and your guests. Being a three and a half hour ferry ride from the port of Piraeus, and a 30 minute flight from Eleftherios Venizelos Airport, Naxos is easily accessible.

Naxos is an island with distinct features, Cycladic beauty, and exquisite but rare scenery. The island offers an array of small romantic white washed churches scattered all over the area. For example, by the seaside, on a mountain top, in a valley, in an orchard or wherever your heart desires. You are sure to find the perfect spot for your wedding on the island of Naxos!

Your guests can admire you up close and personal or from a distance if you choose your ceremony to be held in an isolated area. Another option is in a monastery overlooking the Aegean, where the residents will psalm hymns of joy. On these virgin locations, your eternal vows of love and devotion will be witnessed on a larger scale.
